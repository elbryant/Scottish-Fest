General Information

Dates and Times
The Festival will be held on May 27 and 28, 2017 from 9:00am until 6:00pm at the OC Fair & Event Center (Orange County Fairgrounds), located at 88 Fair Drive in Costa Mesa, California.
Ticket Sales
All ticket sales are final, no refunds for any reason will be given.
The games go on rain or shine.
Massed Bands
The Massed Bands will perform at the opening ceremony on Saturday starting at 12:00 Noon, and at Closing Ceremonies, held on Sunday starting at 5:00pm
Memorial Service
A Memorial Service honoring our veterans will be held at 9:45am on Sunday at the Alex Beaton Stage.
Animals
Absolutely no animals, including dogs, are allowed on the fairgrounds except in the case of Handicap Service Animals or performance dogs. This is a OCFEC rule, not a Festival Rule. If you bring an animal on the grounds, you may be asked to leave by OC Fair and Event Center staff.

Outside Alcohol
No outside alcohol is allowed inside the fairgrounds and cooler checks may be performed. This is a OCFEC rule, not a Festival Rule. If you bring outside alcohol on the grounds, you may be asked to leave by OC Fair and Event Center staff.
Parking Fee
Parking is $8 per car.
